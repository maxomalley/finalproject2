Policies

At Pacific Promise, we believe in finding passionate and dedicated individuals who share our commitment to a cleaner ocean. Our hiring process reflects this philosophy, emphasizing a collaborative and values-driven approach.

Financing

At Pacific Promise, our mission to combat ocean pollution and promote sustainable marine conservation relies on diverse and strategic financing. Our approach combines innovative revenue models, partnerships, and community support to ensure that our initiatives are both impactful and sustainable

Revenue Streams

Pacific Promise offers advanced ocean-cleaning technologies, such as the SeaCycle Filtration Buoy and ReWave Cleanup Drone, designed to remove plastic waste efficiently. A portion of the revenue from these products directly funds research, development, and operational efforts to improve marine environments.

We collaborate with environmentally conscious corporations that share our vision. These partnerships involve sponsorships, co-branded initiatives, and contributions that fuel our mission while helping companies meet their sustainability goals.

As a leader in marine conservation, Pacific Promise secures grants from governments and global environmental organizations. These funds support large-scale cleanup operations, innovative research projects, and community engagement programs.

Our work is also made possible through the generosity of individuals and philanthropists. We run transparent donation campaigns and provide regular updates on how contributions are used to create measurable impacts.

We license our proprietary technologies to organizations worldwide, enabling them to implement our systems locally. This creates a global impact while generating revenue to sustain our operations.

Delivery

At Pacific Promise, our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our products to how we deliver them. Our delivery system is designed to ensure that our cutting-edge ocean cleanup solutions reach our customers efficiently while minimizing environmental impact.

Return Policies

At Pacific Promise, we stand by the quality and effectiveness of our products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return process to ensure your peace of mind.

Eligibility for Returns

Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

The product must be in its original condition, unused, and in its original packaging.

Returns are eligible if the product is defective, damaged during shipping, or does not meet your expectations.
