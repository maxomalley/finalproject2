Products

At Pacific Promise, our products are built with a relentless focus on quality and sustainability. Each item is crafted using cutting-edge technology and eco-friendly materials to ensure durability in even the harshest marine conditions. Our innovative designs prioritize functionality, making cleanup efforts efficient and accessible for individuals and organizations alike. We rigorously test every product to exceed industry standards, guaranteeing reliable performance and long-lasting impact. By combining exceptional craftsmanship with environmental responsibility, Pacific Promise delivers solutions that truly make a difference for our oceans.

WaveNet Drift Catcher

A portable, solar-powered device designed to float on ocean surfaces and capture microplastics. The Drift Catcher uses fine mesh nets to trap particles as small as 0.01 mm, ensuring no plastic escapes. It’s lightweight, durable, and perfect for large-scale cleanup projects or individual use in marinas and bays.

TideGuard EcoBarrier

A deployable barrier system that stops plastic waste from entering the ocean at river mouths. Made from recycled ocean plastics, the EcoBarrier is modular, easy to install, and can withstand extreme weather. It’s an essential tool for preventing plastic from traveling downstream into marine ecosystems.

ReWave Cleanup Drone

An autonomous drone that scans, identifies, and collects floating debris in real time. Equipped with AI and GPS, the ReWave Drone targets high-density pollution areas and collects data for future conservation strategies. Compact and efficient, it’s a modern solution to a modern crisis.

SeaCycle Filtration Buoy

This buoy filters water as it drifts, removing plastic particles and harmful chemicals. Its innovative filter pods are biodegradable and need replacement only once a year. Designed for long-term ocean deployment, the SeaCycle is ideal for both private and governmental cleanup initiatives.
